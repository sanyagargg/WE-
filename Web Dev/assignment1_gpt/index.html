    Early Life

    Sanya Garg was born in India and grew up excelling in her academics. With a natural inclination towards technology, she showed exceptional talent from an early age.

    Education

    Sanya pursued her education at the prestigious Netaji Subhas University of Technology, achieving high academic performance with a 12th Percentage of 92.4 and a 10th Percentage of 96.1.

    Achievements

    Sanya is a Google TalentSprint WE Scholar, NTSE Stage 1 Scholar, and has received numerous other accolades, including being in the top 1% of NSEJS candidates.
